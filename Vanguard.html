SYNOPSIS

Vanguard is a 2020 Chinese action adventure spy-thriller drama film written and directed by Stanley Tong,
starring Jackie Chan, Yang Yang and Miya Muqi. The film was scheduled to be released on 25 January 2020 in
China, but was postponed due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
