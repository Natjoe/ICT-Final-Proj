SYNOPSIS

Forever alone in a crowd, failed comedian Arthur Fleck seeks connection as he walks the streets of
Gotham City. Arthur wears two masks -- the one he paints for his day job as a clown, and the guise he projects
in a futile attempt to feel like he's part of the world around him. Isolated, bullied and disregarded by
society, Fleck begins a slow descent into madness as he transforms into the criminal mastermind known as the Joker.
